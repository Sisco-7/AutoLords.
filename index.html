The Gear Master

Born Sholuade AbdulRoshyd Babajide, Engr. Jidey is the Gear Master at AUTOLORDS, wielding 20+ years of expertise to redefine automatic transmissions in Nigeria.

A YabaTech alumnus 2011, he mastered mechanical engineering and now blends high-performance CNC machines and Bosch diagnostics with unparalleled skill, earning him a titan’s reputation.

From overhauling complex gear systems to crafting bespoke solutions with SolidWorks, Jidey’s AUTOLORDS is a hub of innovation, serving clients with precision and passion.

Mentoring young engineers and pushing technological boundaries, Jidey’s mission is clear: “Master gears, power dreams,” ensuring every vehicle roars back to life.
